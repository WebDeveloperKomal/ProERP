ProERP Learning Channel (SLC) for Partners

The ProERP Learning Channel (SLC) is designed to fully equip our Partners for the success of your and your customer’s business.

  • Home
  • Education

Automotive ProERP business solutions
for Automotive Component manufacturers

Having knowledgeable, experienced partners are our strongest competitive advantage and ProERP is committed to enabling our Partners keep up-to-date and informed about our solutions, so that you are fully equipped with the right knowledge and skills to effectively support your customers.

The ProERP Learning Channel (SLC) provides a complete understanding of the features, facilities and functionality available within the ProERP Solution and how to apply the product across all business environments. The ongoing training, education, and certification you receive through the ProERP Learning Channel (SLC), will help you apply the ProERP solution expertly and confidently, ensuring you are able to provide on-going improvements and the optimization of all aspects of ProERP.

The ProERP Learning Channel (SLC) consists of an Open Learning Library, Self-paced Learning, Certification and Classroom Sessions (physical and virtual classroom):

Open Learning Library

Houses a vast collection of freely available educational content, including:

  • Training guides covering ProERP modules, features, roles, processes, enhancements, and solutions
  • Step-by-step ‘how to’ tutorials
  • Feature demo videos
  • Conceptual videos
  • Recorded webinars
  • Create your own content playlists, download files, and follow content which interests you.

Self-paced Learning

  • Allows you to manage your learning requirements by following a structured learning path or focusing on specific areas where you need additional information
  • Stay up-to-date and informed on the latest ProERP features and functionality
  • Monitor your own progress and compare your progress with others on the leaderboards.

Certifications

These are based on Partner Contact Roles and your grading (Premium or Registered). There are defined Learning Paths for Sales, Pre-Sales, ERP Implementation Consultants, Support Consultants and Project Managers. The PartnerUP registration will provide a number of users’ access to Certifications based on your requirements.​

End-User SLC Plus membership

SLC Plus is a cost-effective annual subscription-based learning platform presented by ProERP. Learners also have the option to utilize the platform on a Pay-As-You-Go basis. Learners can access educational content from a single location and administrators have the ability to track their ongoing performance with ease. It can serve as a consistent and standardized on-boarding platform for your organization and employers can feel rest-assured that their employees receive verified ProERP certifications and are kept up to date with the latest ProERP functionality.

Classroom and Virtual Classroom Sessions (Priced per session)

Browse and sign up for a classroom and virtual classroom sessions available in your region.

ProERP Pay-As-You-Go (PAYG)

PAYG is available to all ProERP partners and customers, regardless if you have purchased an SLC Plus subscription or not. You get to purchase courses online, through a secure payment gateway, that you feel are of relevance to learners that you wish to upskill.​

ProERP Labs

ProERP Labs provides a safe environment in which to practice the utilization of ProERP without impacting on the business live environment. Through the purchase of bundled hours, access a ProERP Lab in your region and upgrade your skillset at your time and at your pace. You can further get access to an instance of ProERP on a test company and be able to let your customers experience the latest ProERP functionality.​

Contact your nearest Regional ProERP Office for more information or find out more about the ProERP Learning Channel.
