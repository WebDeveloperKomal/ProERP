Credit Card Processing ERP Integration

Industry-Leading Payment Gateway

Payment Solutions for ProERP offers real-time credit card processing for the sales order entry and invoicing functions within ProERP. This solution helps ProERP customers improve cash flow and reduce debts by conveniently collecting payments for open invoices via credit cards. This feature works seamlessly with internet sales, sales order processing, as well as the Counter Sales modules. Payment Solutions for ProERP preauthorizes a customer’s purchases against a credit card during the order entry process and bills the credit card at the time of invoicing, including one-time payments or scheduled installments against an account.
