Wholesale distribution software FAQs

What is wholesale distribution software?

Wholesale distribution software is a specialized enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, designed to manage the complex operations and processes involved in the wholesale distribution industry.

Wholesale distribution software streamlines functions such as inventory management, order processing, supply chain logistics, accounting, and customer relationship management, enabling distributors to operate more efficiently and profitably.

Why does my business need wholesale distribution management and accounting software?

As a wholesaler or distributor, you likely deal with a high volume of transactions, complex inventory tracking, and intricate supply chain logistics. Dedicated wholesale distribution management and accounting software can help you:

  • Gain real-time visibility into inventory levels across multiple locations
  • Automate order processing and fulfillment
  • Optimize purchasing and replenishment cycles
  • Manage vendor and customer relationships more effectively
  • Integrate accounting and financial reporting seamlessly
  • Scale operations as your business grows

By implementing the right wholesale accounting software, such as the innovative and powerful solutions from ProERP, you can streamline processes, reduce costs, and improve customer service.

How do I improve wholesale distribution accounting processes?

Accounting for distribution can be time-consuming and confusing because wholesale distributors often deal with accounting complexities like multi-entity management, inter-company transactions, landed cost tracking, and complex revenue recognition scenarios.

Cloud-based wholesale accounting software can dramatically improve accounting efficiency through features like:

  • Automated multi-entity consolidations
  • Customizable dashboards and reporting
  • Seamless integration with CRM and inventory management systems
  • Powerful rules-based revenue recognition engine
  • Robust multi-currency and multi-ledger accounting capabilities

Automating routine accounting tasks for distribution companies and gaining real-time visibility into financial data can significantly enhance accuracy, compliance, and decision-making.

Can wholesale distribution be automated?

Absolutely. Modern wholesale distribution software offers automation capabilities across the entire distribution lifecycle, from sales order entry and inventory replenishment to shipping and invoicing.

Features like barcode scanning, rules-based workflows, and seamless integrations can automate many manual tasks, reducing errors and boosting productivity.

Additionally, machine learning and AI technologies are increasingly being leveraged to automate functions like demand forecasting, route optimization, and predictive maintenance.

How can cloud-based accounting software help wholesale distribution companies?

Cloud-based accounting solutions like Sage Intacct offer several advantages for wholesale distributors:

  • Anytime, anywhere access to real-time financial data from any device
  • Automated software updates and maintenance, reducing IT overhead
  • Scalability to support growth and peak demand periods seamlessly
  • Robust security, backup, and disaster recovery capabilities
  • Easy integration with other cloud applications for inventory, CRM, payroll, etc.
  • Lower upfront costs and predictable subscription-based pricing

By embracing the cloud, wholesale distributors can benefit from increased agility, reduced complexity, and a lower total cost of ownership compared to on-premises systems.

How does wholesale distribution software help me deal with supply chain interruptions?

Disruptions in the supply chain can lead to delays in product delivery, shortages of inventory, and increased costs for distributors. You can use distribution ERP software to help prevent or minimize negative impacts with their collaboration, planning, analysis, and in many cases AI capabilities. Communicating with your suppliers, analyzing their performance data and finally, applying market events to your shipping workstreams will all serve to help you improve your predictive capabilities.
