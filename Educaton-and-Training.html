ERP Training– Become the master of your own technology and Business IT success story

The ongoing ERP training, education and certification your employees can receive through ProERP LearnIt Online will future-proof your company to the benefit of all involved. ProERP LearnIt empowers individual learners to achieve their own desired outcomes and be inspired to become the master of their own technology success story.

The way we experience and interact with the technologically driven world around us continues to change, with the global appetite for technological expertise increasing at the expense of traditional business skills. But one thing remains the same – our desire and capacity to learn, grow and improve ourselves every day. For any business, having well-trained people is a company’s greatest asset, and living and working in today’s digital world is about learning, upskilling and mastering new skills.

Top-of-mind business challenges ProERP LearnIt solves

Learning doesn’t just build human capital, it also builds organizational capital. As employees learn their jobs and the system better, the more proficient they become in their individual roles and more efficiently they can collectively help the business run.

Productivity and efficiency

  • Trained and knowledgeable employees spend less time on troubleshooting and more time on doing business
  • Streamline and optimize business processes
  • Increase user adoption.

Upskilling

  • Ensure your employees’ skillsets stay relevant and show your people you care about their careers and their futures
  • Boost morale – employees who have training and development opportunities are happier in their roles and have a positive outlook on their future within the company
  • Keep your best people! If you don’t provide them with meaningful training, they’ll go to someone who does.

Succession planning

  • Attract, retain, and develop future leaders through well-targeted skills-development efforts
  • Build the bench-strength of your organization in order to ensure its long-term health, growth and stability.

Precise recruiting measures

  • Enable your talent management process by identifying key manufacturing business software roles and mapping out ways to ensure the organization has the right people with the right skills, capabilities, and experiences, in the right place at the right time
  • Provide a benchmark for recruitment. Skills development is one of the most important aspects when considering user-uptake and safeguarding your business IT investment.

Implementation methodology –ProERP IDEAL project administration foundation

All companies invest in ProERP training when they first implement the solution. But training needs arise continuously as your business grows and evolves and as the use of ProERP moves through the next phases, including upgrades, ongoing employee turnover, cross-training and retraining, and ultimately advanced uses of the solution.

A well-trained team that has a solid understanding of ProERP can be confident that they can use the software effectively to:

  • Make informed decisions
  • Deliver timely products and services to their customers.
  • Ensure smooth operations throughout their organization and supply chain
  • Increase productivity and decrease support costs.

A digital learning experience

Training solutions for every role, schedule, and budget

ProERP LearnIt Online combines a digital learning experience as well as instructor-led learning, giving your ProERP users total control of their own learning journey and path to career success. Whatever your ERP business systems and technology level of expertise or career aspirations, ProERP LearnIt Online provides for a fully immersive, intuitive learning experience that brings practical, self-directed education to life. ProERP LearnIt Online learners experience training and development pathways that are designed to be inspiring and easy to use, ensuring the delivery of positive results for your business, your employees and your customers.

ProERP LearnIt Online

ProERP LearnIt Online is a cost-effective annual subscription whereby learners receive access to ProERP courses, and certifications.

  • Learners access courses, and certifications that they may track and work through at their own time and speed
  • Employers or HR professionals can utilize ProERP LearnIt Online as a Learning Management System by being provided with the facility to report on their learners’ education content
  • ProERP LearnIt Online can give learners access to content applicable to them and enrol them in specific programs and courses

Instructor-led Training

For users who are new to ProERP or to a specific area of the software, this training is typically more generic. It focusses more on the transaction flow and is aimed at end users, rather than process owners. Our instructor-led training provides a base-line knowledge of ProERP processes and to enable users to incorporate ProERP into your everyday business.

Led by certified ProERP trainers, these live interactive sessions provide a personal learning experience whether in a virtual classroom or a physical classroom environment.

  • Virtual classroom sessions are held online and led by an instructor. Tutorials are provided so you can practice on your test environment. Recordings of virtual sessions are provided after the course ends
  • Physical classroom sessions are held in ProERP offices. An instructor leads you through the concepts and you work through hands-on exercises using laptops we provide, and which are loaded with our bicycle company training data.

ProERP Labs

Hosted in Microsoft Azure, customers get access via remote desktop to a ProERP Lab in their region, where they will experience a virtual instance of ProERP’s latest release. Customers purchase bundled hours for usage by their learners, and if they run out of allocated hours, they can top these up.

ProERP Labs enables users to:

  • Practice utilizing ProERP in a safe environment
  • Upskill themselves on ProERP without impacting the business live environment
  • Engage with additional features and functionality of ProERP outside of the business environment
  • Gives access to an instance of ProERP if no test company is available at the customer.

PAYG

Shop LearnIt allows anyone interested in learning about the ProERP product to purchase one or more courses. They can also view related courses based on topic or business function. Customers who have purchased a subscription, may want to purchase additional courses for users on a pay-as-you-go basis to supplement the learning.

Once users purchase courses, they will have access to them from ProERP LearnIt Online. Users can personalize their profile and keep track of their progress and achievements.

Users will have access to their courses for 60 days from their last purchase, and the record of learning will still be retained.
