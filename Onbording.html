FAQs

Roadmap to your ProERP PartnerUP journey

How does ProERP onboard partners?

ProERP has invested heavily in making sure that our partners have a smooth journey when joining ProERP, from personalized tracks we ensure you are brought up-to-speed in what matters to you.

How long do partners stay with ProERP?

We are proud to have Partners who have been with us for over 40 years, and with a 98% customer retention ratio we love our ProERP community which continues to grow from strength to strength.

How long does partner certification take?

    This is dependent on which tracks you take – with our ProERP LearnIt Online platform you have access to everything you may need. Once you are up-to-date on the online platform we courage in-person sessions to further assist you get enabled.

What is the time to partner ROI?

    Our Partners have seen ROI as soon as 3 months after joining the ProERP PartnerUP program.

Who are the key ISVs?

    We have an extensive program of ISV providers – to view them and learn more about their offering view our ISV section
